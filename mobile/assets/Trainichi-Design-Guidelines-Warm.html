03 — Spacing

Space & Rhythm

Generous breathing room creates a sense of calm. An 8px base unit ensures consistency.

xxs 4px Icon gaps, tight elements
xs 8px Inline spacing, small gaps
sm 16px Default padding, form gaps
md 24px Card padding, sections
lg 32px Section dividers
xl 48px Page margins, hero areas
xxl 64px Major sections
