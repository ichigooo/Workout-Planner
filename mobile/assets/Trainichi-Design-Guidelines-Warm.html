03 — Spacing

Space & Rhythm

Generous breathing room creates a sense of calm. An 8px base unit ensures consistency.

xs 4px Icon gaps, tight elements
sm 8px Inline spacing, small gaps
md 16px Default padding, form gaps
lg 24px Card padding, sections
xl 32px Section dividers
2xl 48px Page margins, hero areas
3xl 64px Major sections

Border Radius

Soft, organic shapes that feel natural and inviting.

sm
8px — Buttons, badges
md
12px — Inputs, small cards
lg
20px — Cards, panels
full
50% — Avatars, pills
